Interested in Joining Our Physician Network?

The Quality Alliance (QA) is one of the largest clinically integrated networks in the nation, spanning more than 21 counties in Northeast Ohio. As an integrated provider network comprised of independent physician practices and employed Cleveland Clinic physicians, we collaborate on quality measures that focus on chronic disease management and the challenges of rising healthcare costs.
