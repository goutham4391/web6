Why Give to Cleveland Clinic

As a nonprofit healthcare organization, Cleveland Clinic is grateful for your support. Your gift to Cleveland Clinic will help us to provide the highest-quality, compassionate care, conduct lifesaving research and offer leading-edge medical education. Whether you’re making a gift to honor a loved one or providing seed funding for innovative projects through a gift to our Caregiver Catalyst Grants, your support advances our efforts to find the cures the world needs now.

Supporting Cancer Patients at Cleveland Clinic

Supporting Cancer Patients at Cleveland Clinic

At Cleveland Clinic, exceptional cancer care is complemented by a wide range of services that go beyond the medical needs of patients – addressing their emotional, mental and financial needs. These important services, which are not covered by insurance, are free to patients, thanks to the generosity of supporters who want to enhance our patients’ well-being.
