Referring a Patient to Cleveland Clinic

How do I refer a patient to Cleveland Clinic?

To refer a patient to a Cleveland Clinic location in Ohio, please print and fill out our referral form and fax to 216.448.9737 (Attention: Referring Physician Hotline). You can also refer a patient by phone using our Referring Physician Hotline at 855.REFER.123 (855.733.3712). Our team is available 24/7 for any questions you have.

How do I refer a patient to Cleveland Clinic Florida or Nevada?
 To learn more about our referral process in Florida or Nevada, please visit the pages below:

  • For Florida referrals
  • For Nevada referrals

Please contact our Referring Physician Hotline, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for all your needs, by calling 855.REFER.123 (855.733.3712).

You can contact our Referring Physician Hotline for help with all your needs, including:

  • Obtaining information on our clinical specialists and services.
  • Scheduling and confirming patient appointments.
  • Assistance in the resolution of any service-related issues.
  • Connecting with Cleveland Clinic specialists.

When contacting us to schedule your patients, please have the following information readily available:

  • Patient demographic information.
  • Reason for visit.
  • Any special needs that will require facilitation once the patient arrives.
  • Patient insurance information, if available.

We will work with your patient to complete registration process and to schedule an appointment at the patient's convenience. You will be notified once the appointment is scheduled.

Physician Relations

Physician Relations

Cleveland Clinic’s Physician Relations team is responsible for continually addressing the needs of our customers – referring physicians and their office staff.

Our physician liaisons serve as your personal connection to Cleveland Clinic specialists and services, and are available to schedule personal office visits; address questions, concerns and issues; and much more.

When should you contact Physician Relations?

Please call 855.REFER.123 (855.733.3712), and ask to speak with a physician liaison, to:

  • Obtain information on our clinical specialists and services
  • Receive assistance in the resolution of any service-related issues
  • Schedule Grand Rounds or obtain information on Continuing Medical Education
  • Request to meet with a Cleveland Clinic specialist

Our physician liaisons are available to referring physicians and their office staff to discuss how we can better serve you and help meet your patient’s needs. Please call us at 855.REFER.123 (855.733.3712) or email [email protected].
