Paperless Billing

What to Expect from Paperless Billing

As of Sept. 1, 2022, we’re moving to paperless billing. What does that mean? If you’re a MyChart user, you’ll automatically be switched over to this secure, convenient and eco-friendly way to get your bill. You will find and can pay your monthly statements in your MyChart account. If you’re not a fan of paperless billing, you can switch back to paper statements after Sept. 1 in your MyChart account in the MyChart Mobile App or on the MyChart website.
