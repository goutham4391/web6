Main Campus Entrances During COVID-19

In an effort to reduce the potential spread of COVID-19 and protect patients, permitted visitors, caregivers and the community, we are limiting entrances into our hospital at Cleveland Clinic main campus.

Self Park Discount Coupons

If you expect frequent visits to the campus, self-park discount coupon options are available. Purchase self-park discount coupon options from:

  • All Valet cashier desks (located in J1, A1, CA1, i1, Q1, R1)
  • Parking Services Office (open Mon. – Fri., 7 a.m. - 4:30 p.m., JJ North Office Basement)
Time of stay Rate
5-Day Passes (5 Tickets) $30.00
7-Day Unlimited Pass $30.00
30-Day Unlimited Pass $100.00

Coupons are valid for use in self-park facilities only (not accepted at a valet).
