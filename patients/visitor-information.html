Visiting Our Main Campus

Travel to Cleveland Clinic Main Campus Map
Questions: 216.444.2200
A Visitor’s Guide to Main Campus

Every time you walk through our doors, we will treat you and your loved ones like a part of our larger family. It’s our goal to provide you with the best possible experience while you’re with us.

Whether you’re here for yourself, a family member or a loved one, we want you to know that we are here for you.

This guide is here to help you navigate our main campus and its many services. Here, you’ll find information about our services — parking, transportation, food and places to stay the night — to name just a few. We’ll also provide some important phone numbers. And tell you about measures we take to keep our patients and caregivers safe.

While you’re with us, there are also things to see outside of any appointments or procedures. From nearby attractions to our art tours, we’ll connect you to experiences that you can enjoy while visiting Cleveland.

If you have any questions, please call Cleveland Clinic’s Concierge Desk at 216.636.1288 from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.

Thank you for visiting our main campus and coming to us for your healthcare needs.

About Cleveland

Activities Around Main Campus

Feel free to explore main campus during your visit. We have spots to relax, activities to try and beautiful art to see.

ATMs & Banking

Our main campus has nine conveniently located ATMs, as well as other nearby banking options.

Labs

Our full-service, nationally recognized laboratories perform tests quickly and accurately.

Lodging

We have both short-term and long-term options available for your stay — including hotels and other specialized accommodations.

Mail, Fax, Phones & Wi-Fi

Whether you need to use a phone, send a fax or piece of mail, we can help you. We also have free Wi-Fi available for you to use during your stay.

Ombudsman Office

At Cleveland Clinic, your satisfaction is very important to us. Our representatives will listen to your concerns and do what we can to help whenever possible.
